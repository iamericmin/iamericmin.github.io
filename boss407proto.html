BOSS 407 PROTO

PRIMARY OBJECTIVE: 32-BIT ARM DESIGN

Basically, an Over-engineered Super Sampler. I know, weird name.

The Boss 407 is an STM32 board I've designed around the STM32F407VET6. It's really nothing special, just a minimal circuit for the chip and some LEDs. It doesn't even have all the IO pins broken out.
