CHERRY BOMB

2020/02/05
This is just an idea in my head for now. The Cherry Bomb will be a Parallax Propeller dev board. I will start working on this when I have a working Rocket Panda.

I got the name from a random YouTube video I stumbled upon about a custom '68 camaro. The Camaro was treated with a two-tone finish of glossy red and matte black. The car was named Cherry Bomb and I thought it was a cute name for a project. The PCB will be red and white.
