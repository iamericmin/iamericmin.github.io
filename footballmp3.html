FOOTBALL GAME AUDIO PLAYER

An idea to convert my Mattel Classic Football game into an MP3 player while keeping its game functionality and retro aesthetic. Only a simple concept at the moment. I have a simple idea and nothing else. Will probably utilize an Arduino MP3 decoder module, tactile momentary switches, high-quality machined volume knob, and a rugged 3.5mm female audio jack.
