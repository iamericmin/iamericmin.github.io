GIGA WORKBENCH

PCB DESIGN

Wanted Propeller Professional Development Board.

Didn't have Propeller Professional Development Board.

Propeller Professional Development Board was discontinued.

Made my own Propeller Professional Development Board.


The "Alphanumeric photon emitters" on the top-right corner of the board is actually an Adafruit I2C 14-segment featherwing. I decided to use a featherwing instead of multiplexing LEDs to save pins and assembly time. On the far right I put some JST-PH connectors. They were meant to be I2C connectors but in the schematic they aren't connected to any signals :)

I believe the quote for this board was around a grand for a batch of ten PCBs. That made me lose any hopes of making it a reality. Luckily, after many days of rooting around the internet, I finally found a used Propeller Pro Dev Board for sale. I instantly bought it and walked my first steps with the Parallax Propeller. I'll leave you with the schematic for the Giga Workbench!

I know the schematic looks like a nuclear warzone, but hey it was the best I could do in 2017.
