THE TIME MACHINE MK. IV

The Time Machine Mk. 4 is my second major project after the Time Machine Mk. 2. It is a homebrew digital wristwatch powered by a 12mm coin cell battery and a Nordic NRF52832 SoC. It is a huge leap forward from the Time Machine Mk. 2. It is smaller, more energy efficient(but a lower battery life since it uses a 50mah coin cell instead of a 350mah lipo battery), and in my opinion, much better-looking.
