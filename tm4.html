TIME MACHINE MK. IV

The Time Machine Mk. 4 is my second major project after the Time Machine Mk. 2. It is a homebrew digital wristwatch powered by a 12mm coin cell battery and a Nordic NRF52832 SoC. It is a huge leap forward from the Time Machine Mk. 2. It is smaller, more energy efficient(but a lower battery life since it uses a 50mah coin cell instead of a 350mah lipo battery), and in my opinion, much better-looking.
First, let's get into the design. I designed the watch after Iron Man and War Machine's armor designs in Avengers: Infinity War. For example, the big "004" on the bottom left corner of the watch is also present in Rhodes's armor on his right bicep. I thought it looked pretty cool, and I think it fits on my watch perfectly.

I placed 5 blue surface-mount LEDs to mimic the 5 arc reactors on the chest of the Mark 50 armor. It gives the watch a nice cyberpunk vibe while maintaining a symmetrical look. Plus, the LEDs can be used as indicator lights or simply as bling.
