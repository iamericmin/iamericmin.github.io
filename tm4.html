THE TIME MACHINE MK. IV

The Time Machine Mk. 4 is my second major project after the Time Machine Mk. 2. It is a homemade digital wristwatch that is powered by a Nordic nRF52832 SoC and a 12mm coin-cell battery. I don't want to bore you out, so I'll go straight into the specs.

  • Processor: nRF52832 Soc. 64MHz ARM cortex M4 with 64k RAM and 512k flash.
  • Power: 12mm coin-cell battery. About three days of battery life.
  • Display: If you can call it a display. Dual 7-segment LEDs with five auxiliary LEDs.
  • Input: Triple momentary tactile switches.
  • Dimensions: 43.18mm Width and Length, 10.2mm Thickness.
  • Strap: 22mm NATO strap.
