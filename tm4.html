ERIC MIN

TIME MACHINE MK. IV

Board and schematic design as of Spooktober 12 2018. Still deciding whether to just skip the Mark III and manufacture these. It uses a low-power BLE chip with the Espruino JS frimware built-in. It runs on a CR1220 coin cell battery which I guesstimate would give it about a week's worth of battery life. Best of all, it eliminates the former model's USB port which (finally) means it can have a perfectly symmetrical design! I took design cues from the War Machine armors in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
FEATURE LIST
  • Micro joystick with tactile switch
  • BLE fuctionality for wireless applications
  • Four indicator LEDs
  • improved time interpreter and menu system with built-in programs
  • 1-voice beeper speaker for simple sound and alarm functionality
  • CR1220 coin cell power + battery stick port
  • symmetrical design
