TIME MACHINE MK. V

Here at Min Industries, we never settle on "good enough." We always strive to be greater than we are. The Time Machine Mk. V is the product of our sleepless nights, our mugs of coffee, and our burning desire to be the very best. Our magnum opus.

The Time Machine Mk. V is an upgraded version of the Time Machine Mk. IV. It uses the same processor, but adds in a bigger battery, a serial port, and a debug port. The battery is a CR2032 which is four times larger than the Mark IV's CR1225 battery. The larger battery allows for brighter LEDs and a lowder piezo speaker. The serial and debug port allows for easy firmware upgrades with Adafruit's custom DFU bootloader. Yes, the firmware for the Mark V will be completely re-written in C for lower energy consumption and faster execution. Anyways, here's the tea.

SPEC LIST

  • PROCESSOR: nRF52832 Soc. 64MHz ARM cortex M4 with 64k RAM and 512k flash.
  • FIRMWARE: Adafruit custom DFU bootlaoder + C firmware
  • POWER: CR2032 coin cell battery. Estimated battery life of around a month.
  • DISPLAY: Dual 7-segment LEDs with five auxiliary LEDs.
  • INPUT: Triple momentary tactile switches.
  • SOUND: Mini piezo speaker.
  • DIMENSIONS: 43.18mm width and length, 8.55mm thickness(1.65mm thinner than the Mark IV!).
  • STRAP: 22mm NATO strap.
